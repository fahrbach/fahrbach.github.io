About

I am a Research Scientist at Google NYC. I recieved my PhD from the Georgia Institute of Technology while supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship under the supervision of Dana Randall. My research interests include randomized algorithms and data structures, Markov chain Monte Carlo and problems from statistical physics, large-scale graph mining and optimization, and enumerative combinatorics.
