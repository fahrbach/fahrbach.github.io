About

I am a Research Scientist at Google in the Algorithms and Optimization group. I recieved my PhD from the Georgia Institute of Technology under the supervision of Dana Randall while supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. My research interests are in algorithms, discrete mathematics, machine learning, and optimization.
